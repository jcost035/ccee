Student Programs

When students go through hands-on experiences, they come away with unforgettable memories that make learning even more powerful. Explore CCEE’s student programs below.

2nd-5th grade

Never Too Young

The earlier students learn about important concepts such as budgeting, savings, and investing, the better. Students learn all about these themes through our after-school program, Never Too Young.

6th-12th grade

National Economics Challenge

The National Economics Challenge is a great way for students to flex their intellectual muscle & test their economic content knowledge by competing with other students from across the state.

6th-12th grade

Personal Finance Challenge

The National Personal Finance Challenge is an opportunity for students to learn about personal finance by connecting with other students from across the state in an online competition. The top scoring teams are invited to the state championships where they compete for cash prizes!

9th-12th grade

The Adviser’s Contest

The Adviser’s Contest provides California’s high school students with the opportunity to collaborate with an assigned a mentor– who is typically a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) or professional with a background in finance–and a fictional client case study Over 4 weeks, students prepare a presentation with their mentor in anticipation of the virtual competition day

Couple student contests with our professional development programs for an even richer instructional experience.
