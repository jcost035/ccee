How the program works:

Developed by leading practitioners working hand-in-hand with teachers, The $martPath Platform provides simple, narrative units that bring economic and financial education to life in your classroom. $martPath lessons and levels teach students about personal financial responsibility (such as saving, prudent spending, and prioritizing needs and wants) through grade-appropriate, curriculum-aligned, interactive lessons.

The CCEE $martPath program is easily completed in three steps:
Step One

Participate in the hour-long $martPath training session and receive an initial $50 teacher stipend

Step Two

Participate in the hour-long $martPath training session and receive an initial $50 teacher stipend

Step Three

Receive a second $50 teacher stipend upon submission of students’ pre and post test results
