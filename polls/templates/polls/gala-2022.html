ABOUT THE EVENT

Join us for a night of cocktails, hors d'oeuvres, and fun at the beautiful Skirball Cultural Center as we celebrate the "Golden Opportunity" to give every student in California the chance to succeed!
At CCEE, we still believe that the chance to build wealth is possible for everyone, as long as they are given the resources and tools to prosper. 2020 exposed some of the deepest inequities of our systems, and we responded with new programs and initiatives to fix what is broken.
With this new normal, we've been given a “Golden Opportunity” - the ability to grow our work to reach every student within every canyon and mountain town across California.
Our goal is for our programs to finally cover the entire state so that every family in California has a shot at upward mobility. As we train our teachers, we know we are empowering thousands of students and their families.
This work could not be possible without the help of our incredible partners like YOU who are committed to bringing those who have been traditionally marginalized into the fold.
