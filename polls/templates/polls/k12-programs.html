Educator Programs

We know that when teachers feel supported, their students succeed. Economic and financial literacy is the gateway to opportunity and mobility, and you have the power to make that happen for your students right in your very own classroom! CCEE’s programs are designed to meet you where you’re at, so you can feel confident in teaching engaging and relevant economics and personal finance. Browse our programs below to learn more and save your spot for upcoming dates.

1st-8th grade, SPED

Economics Teacher Certification Program

Together with the California Association of School Economics Teachers, CCEE hosts a multi-day seminar where educators who are teaching California’s high school economics come together to learn engaging instructional strategies to bring back to their students.

1st-8th grade, SPED

Federal Reserve Institute

The Federal Reserve Institute is hosted in San Diego for a week-long immersive program where teachers learn directly from the Federal Reserve Bank’s economic educators.

1st-8th grade, SPED

Developed by leading practitioners working hand-in-hand with teachers, The $martPath Platform provides simple, fun lessons that bring economic and financial education to life in your classroom. $martPath units teach students about personal financial responsibility (such as saving, prudent spending, and prioritizing needs and wants) through grade-appropriate, curriculum-aligned, interactive lessons.

ALL GRADES

CCEE’s interactive workshops support teachers efforts to deliver the most engaging and relevant economics and personal finance content to their students.
